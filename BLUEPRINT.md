# Affiliate Director AI Blueprint

## Status
Draft v1.0

## Vision
Affiliate Director AI adalah platform SaaS berbasis AI yang membantu UMKM, affiliate marketer, dan content creator membuat konten pemasaran secara cepat melalui Dashboard, AI Workflow Engine, Make.com, dan Result Studio.

## Architecture

Dashboard
↓
AI Workflow Engine
↓
Make.com
↓
Result Studio

## Development Principles

- MVP First
- Modular Architecture
- Blueprint Driven Development
- One Sprint at a Time
- No Breaking Changes Without Approval

---
Blueprint ini akan terus diperbarui pada setiap sprint pengembangan.
